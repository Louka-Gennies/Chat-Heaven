Privacy Policy and Data Protection

Chat Heaven (hereinafter "the Forum") values the protection of your personal data and your privacy. Data Retention We retain your personal data for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this privacy policy or to comply with legal obligations. 7. Your Rights In accordance with the GDPR, you have the following rights: Right of Access: you can request access to your personal data that we hold. Contact If you have any questions about this privacy policy or your personal data, you can contact us at chatheaven@gmail.com. This privacy policy is effective as of 19/06/2024.
